About Us

WHERE CHAMPIONS
ARE MADE

Three Star Gym is Kathmandu's trusted fitness destination, located at Loktantrik Chowk, Manamaiju — right beside Angel Mart micro station. We offer state-of-the-art equipment, certified personal training, Zumba classes, and expert nutritional guidance in a clean, welcoming environment built for all fitness levels.

From beginners to seasoned athletes, our community-driven approach and knowledgeable staff ensure every member gets the support they need to reach their goals.
